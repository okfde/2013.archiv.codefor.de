--- layout: lab key: berlin lang: en --- Welcome to the Berlin OK lab! We are about 30 people working in various groups on projects around open data and civic tech. We come from all different areas, so the projects in which we work on are very diverse. We meet every Monday at 19.00 O'clock at Wikimedia ( Tempelhofer Ufer 23) to work on our projects. On the 2nd Monday of the month, we also host an idea round to show event participants our previous projects and new ideas and projects that can be contributed to. If this has sparked your interest, feel free to swing by one of our intro meetings.
